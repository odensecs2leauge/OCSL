About

The OCSL Grassroot Series is a community Counter-Strike league based in Odense. It is designed for local teams, simple competition, and a fun offline finals event. All funding is community-based and fully transparent.

Qualifier System

Qualifiers require a small team fee. If your team does not reach the group stage, you receive a full refund. If your team reaches the group stage, the fee is used to help fund the event.

Fees

Team Fee 10 kr
Spectator Ticket 10 kr

Refund Policy

Teams that fail to qualify for the group stage will be refunded their qualifier fee. Refunds are sent directly to the team captain via MobilePay.

MobilePay

All payments are handled through a MobilePay Box. Only the organizer has access to payouts. The Box includes two categories: Team Fee and Spectator Ticket.

Event Format

  • Online Qualifiers
  • Group Stage
  • Playoffs
  • Trophy for the winning team (if funding allows)

Contact

For questions or sign-ups, contact the organizer on Discord.
